Parameters
idUnique identificator of load order of tasks.
task_metaVariable containing further info about task. It's required that cmd_args entry has just 2 arguments - current and new name of the file. The semantics is same as POSIX rename() function, see http://pubs.opengroup.org/onlinepubs/000095399/functions/rename.html or http://www.boost.org/doc/libs/1_59_0_b1/libs/filesystem/doc/reference.html#rename for more info.
